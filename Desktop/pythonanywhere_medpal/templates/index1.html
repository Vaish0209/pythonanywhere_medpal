So, to tackle all these issues, we have designed this website to help people. Our website is a bi-product of introducing technology in the medical field. It is a mere application of AI.

We aim at :

1.Helping people track their health so that it reduces untimely death and leads to healthy lifestyle.

2.Educating people regarding the significance of proper diet and also exercises.

3.Reduces fee expenses.
